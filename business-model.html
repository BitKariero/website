Business Model

Proposal

We propose splitting our operations into two distinct units. One will run the core infrastructure, that is the protocol and CA cert lists, as an open and non-profit foundation. The other will provide value-added services on top of that in a for-profit manner.

BitKariero is sustainable both as a for-profit and non-profit project

Sustainability as a Non-Profit Project

Running the core system as a non-profit will be financially feasible since it will use a decentralised blockchain with no inherit need for costly hosting. Being open to all, as long as our technology is not surpassed, a for-profit company has no reason to clone the infrastructure â€“ unless they want to create a walled-garden. Not having ours as a walled garden will make it more enticing for third parties to add integrations for it and to potentially contribute to the core. For the CA list we would use a similar process to the Mozilla CA Certificate Policy to add third parties in a fair and consistent manner.

Sustainability as a For-Profit Project

As a for-profit we would provide an end user interface and hosting solution, whereby customers can have their own BitKariero profile hosted by us. On this profile they could include their verified references and records from the blockchain, and we will verify them and list them online in a manner that anyone can access without having to verify it themselves. Working off this, we could also provide tools for recruiters and companies to comb through these profiles. The for-profit will also provide APIs and work with other companies to implement integrations, and charge a commission for this work.

We see a bright future for BitKariero as a for-profit project
